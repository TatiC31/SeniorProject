Tech Takeover

Technology has taken over the world and the new generation. It has become more and more popular in today's day of age. With the rise of technology people have grown a severe addiction to it. Using technology and social media sites has become the new normal. used to communicate to friends and family, used in healthcare, transportation, education etc.. Technology has impacted everybody's lives in different ways.

Tech Obession

Technology addiction is very real. The meaning behind technology addiction is “when an obsessive and impulsive use of technology causes negative consequences to the user.” (“Technology Addiction Treatment in Austin TX.”) Technology addiction is not so common in older people but definitely common with the new generation. Many mental health experts have noticed that the percentage of technology users use technology in a “compulsive and out-of-control manner.” (“What You Need to Know About Internet Addiction.”

Addiction & Gambling

Being addicted to technology and social media sites is like being addicted to gambling. Social media sites and gambling use the same reward schedule. This reward schedule that is being use in gambling and on social media sites was created by B.F Skinner and Charles Fester (Miller). B.F Skinner was a psychologist known for his “impact on behaviorism”(Cherry). The reason why gambling is so addicting is because of this certain reward schedule. Gambling triggers the reward system in the part of the brains which is why people cannot pull away from the slot, which is similar to social media sites and technology. Instead of not pulling away from the slot is the same way how people are with technology. People see something that catches their attention on their feed and keep scrolling and scrolling and end up in a rabbit hole.
